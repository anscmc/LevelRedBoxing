Name: Kiersten Jungling
Nickname: KK (soccer nickname)
Life quote/moto: "If people are doubting how far you'll go, go so far that you can't hear them anymore."
Guilty pleasure: Fried food, dancing when no one is watching, watching sappy love movies.

Meet Kiersten Jungling (soon to be Mrs. Bacon). "When asked, 'What is the most interesting thing about yourself?' I always say... I am adopted. Being adopted has shaped who I am and helped me become the person I want to be and continue to strive to be". She loves being an athlete and being outdoors. She currently lives with her fiance where they have 40 acres. They have a walker coon hound named Diesel and a calico kitty named Marley. "I have a step son who I absolutely adore. He is seven and has taught me so much".

"I love being a trainer! My clients are worth everything to me because I get the greatest satisfaction seeing them succeed. Whether it be pushing them past their breaking point only to have them realize how strong they feel, to seeing them achieve goals they never thought possible. And that is the same way I feel about being a Level Red instructor! I have so much fun pushing everyone as hard as I can and pushing myself to keep everyone on their toes. I strive to be the best trainer and instructor that each and every member deserves."
