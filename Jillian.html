JILLIAN

Name: Jillian Gentile
Nickname: JillyG, JBomb
Life quote/moto: "Small goals produce big transformations!"
Guilty pleasure: Late night eating EVERYTHING!

Jillian has a sassy, sarcastic personality and a lot of energy! She motivates her classes by being "tough with a smile". She started her group fitness journey as an iRock member in 2013 and started instructing there in 2016 because of her loud mouth and energetic personality. Outside of the club she stays busy being a fur mama of 3 and loving wife in the small town of Waterford. She enjoys many outdoor adventures including biking and kayaking. She's a social butterfly and is always making plans with her friends and family. "I'd rather be busy than bored" has been influential to her especially when it comes to group fitness! "Group fitness classes have held me more accountable to sticking with a healthier lifestyle! It's kept motivated and moving and can help anyone keep with their workout routines!"
