KAITLYNNE

Name: Kaitlynne Prozan
Nickname: Kaito
Life quote/moto: "Nothing is real if you don't believe in who you are." -Rocky Balboa
Guilty pleasure: Naps, anything on the Taco Bell menu

Kaitlynne is a pint-sized Tomboy with a friendly, quirky sense of humor despite her "resting gym face". She's a big kid at heart, and her favorite show of all time is The Simpsons. She's a registered nurse who firmly believes that if she's going to care for and educate her patients about clean/healthy living, she ought to practice what she preaches. In addition to her love of working out, she loves training martial arts regularly. She currently holds a second degree black belt in Combat Hapkido, and continues to train and teach the art at a local martial arts studio. When not at the gym, Kaitlynne likes to unwind by going to the movies, antique shopping with her husband, spending time with her friends and family, or just lounging around the house with her talkative Siamese cat, Mr. Jinx.
